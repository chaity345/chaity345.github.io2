"Fukuoka" in JAPAN

Fukuoka is well connected by both rail and air. The prefecture has multiple stops on both the JR Kyushu Shinkansen and the JR Sanyo Shinkansen, which links to Hiroshima, Osaka, Kyoto and Tokyo.
Fukuoka Airport is extremely close to the city center. Fukuoka City’s Hakata Port offers ferries to South Korea and to many of Kyushu’s smaller islands.
